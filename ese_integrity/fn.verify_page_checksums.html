pub fn verify_page_checksums(db: &EseDatabase) -> Vec<EseStructuralAnomaly>
Expand description

Verify the Vista+ ECC-32 XOR page checksum for every data page in db.

Pages where both the column-parity field ([0..4]) and the XOR field ([4..8]) are zero are treated as unchecked (empty/synthetic pages) and skipped silently.

For all other pages the correct algorithm is applied:

  • logical_pgno = page_number - 1
  • csum = logical_pgno XOR (every 4-byte word in the entire page)
  • A mismatch against the stored XOR at bytes [4..8] produces [PageChecksumMismatch].

This algorithm was confirmed correct against 326/326 non-empty pages in the chainsaw SRUDB fixture (Python probe, 2026-05-20).
